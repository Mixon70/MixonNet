Taking Action: A Journey of Faith and Fulfillment

Bless your heart, dear sister in Christ, as a busy mom homeschooling three precious children, active in church, and caring for your home down here in Kingsville, Texas, you are already doing so much. Yet, you feel a calling to explore a side hustle that can grow into something more—whether it's a course, a Bible study, a recipe blog, or whatever the Lord's laid on your heart. With faith and action, your goals are closer than a Sunday sermon. Let's explore some sweet, attainable options together.

Creating a Course or Bible Study

Share your God-given gifts by creating an online course or Bible study. Whether it's teaching homeschooling tricks or diving deep into scripture, your heart for Jesus shines through. Platforms like Teachable make it as easy as pie to start.

Writing a Recipe Blog

Love whipping up meals for your family? Turn those recipes into a blog sweeter than Texas pecan pie. Share your faith through stories and suppers. With WordPress or Blogger, you can start writing faster than you can say "amen."

Selling Printables on Etsy

Create digital treasures like Bible study guides, recipe cards, or homeschooling helpers. Once they're listed, they can bring in blessings while you sleep. Tools like Canva make it simpler than a summer Sunday.

Reflect on Your Gifts

Take a quiet moment—maybe with a glass of sweet tea—and think about your talents. Do you love teaching, writing, or creating? How can you use these to bless others and honor God? These side hustles fit right into your life like a hymn in church.

"I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me." - Philippians 4:13

Remember, darlin', you are capable and loved beyond measure. Taking that first step is the start of a beautiful journey, and with Jesus holding your hand, you can do mighty things. May the Lord bless you and keep you as you chase this dream.
